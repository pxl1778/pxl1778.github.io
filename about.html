Peter Lockhart

I'm a sophomore Game Design and Development major at the Rochester Institute of Technology. I grew up in Rochester and video games have always been a large part of my life. After taking Computer Science classes in high school, I knew that programming and game development were going to be what I do with my life.

In game development, I am primarily a programmer. I enjoy thinking of mechanics and how to implement them in a fun and creative way. Recently, I have participated in a game jam and a hackathon and I've found that I really enjoy working in small group of people to program a lot in a small amount of time.

I'm inspired by games that have a deep narrative and involved story. Games such as Bioshock, Shadow of the Colossus, Final Fantasy, and Journey. These are the kinds of games I'd love to make. I want to create an emotional and thought-provoking experience for players that will be memorable.

I'm currently gaining a better understanding of Unity, learning 3D modeling and animation in Maya, and improving on web front end programming.
