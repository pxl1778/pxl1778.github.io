SwitchBlade

SwitchBlade is a top-down, action, arena game made in Monogame C# based around a mechanic that when you die, your character drops a sword that is then picked up by an enemy that you start playing as. This was a group project with three other people.

I worked mostly on the user interface elements as well as the structure of navigating between them. I also worked on some of the mechanics of the gameplay and created all of the art assets and animations. We all learned a lot about working in groups and I found it to be an enjoyable experience.
